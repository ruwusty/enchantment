Welcome to the new DataSoc blog! We've moved from Google Slides embeds to a proper publishing platform so you can actually *read* our content without squinting at a presentation iframe.

## What to Expect

Our blog covers a wide range of topics across the data world:

- **Machine Learning** - from the fundamentals to cutting-edge research
- **Data Visualisation** - telling stories with data
- **Ethics in AI** - the conversations we all need to have
- **Career advice** - breaking into the industry as a student
- **Conference recaps** - what we learned and who we met

## Get Involved

Are you a DataSoc member who wants to write? Reach out to the Education Subcommittee - we're always looking for contributors. Whether it's a deep dive into a paper you loved, a tutorial, or an opinion piece, we want to hear from you.

Find us at [hello@unswdata.com](mailto:hello@unswdata.com) or through any of our socials.
